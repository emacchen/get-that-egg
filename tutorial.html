Movement Key Purpose
Up W To Move Up
Down S To Move Down
Right D To Move Right
Left A To Move Left
Attack Space/Left Click To Attack Enemies (Or your friends)
Dash Shift/Right Click To Dash
Use Item V To Use The Various Items You'll Acquire
Cycle Inventory Up Key To Cycle
Cycle Inventory Down Down Key To Cycle Downwards
Interact Shift To Interact With The Many NPCs
Throw Shift (When In Hand) To Throw Various Objects
Text Choose WASD To Choose Dialogue
Text Select Space To Select Dialogue
Pause Esc To Pause
See Quest F To See Your Current Quest
